about

The corner of Broadway & Covert St

Covert Coffee sits in a four-story brick building that went up between 1888 and 1889 — back when this block was still the Covert family farm. Since then it's been a grocery empire, a fruit stand, a luncheonette, a corner bodega, and now a coffee shop.

Right where Bushwick bleeds into Bed-Stuy. The kind of spot you stumble into once and keep coming back to.

"I was born and brought up in Brooklyn and am proud of it and always found it a beautiful place. We had no crime, murder and other badness. We were taught to love God and our neighbors."

— Mrs. Edgar A. Steup, Woodhaven · Brooklyn Daily Eagle, Feb 1945
