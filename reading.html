The Great Bridge
David McCullough · 1972
The epic story of the building of the Brooklyn Bridge. This work chronicles how the Brooklyn Bridge was constructed between 1869 and 1883, transforming from an impossibility destined to fail into an engineering triumph. The narrative follows chief engineer Washington Roebling and his wife Emily as they navigate both technical obstacles and political corruption to complete this iconic American structure.
